Profile

I've made it my goal this term to learn how to communicate and connect with people on a deeper level. I'm taking Public Speaking as my elective, I've joined UW Toastmasters club, and I'm also studying effective communication in my free time.

While I've been spending my teenage years sharpening my technical skills, I feel that I've lost touch with other people. In order to truly benefit others by writing software, I will have to understand their problems and connect with them.

In this discipline, it is vital to be able to articulate your ideas, and empathize with others. To me, software is the most efficient way to communicate your ideas, and the easiest way to create tools for people. This is why I have dedicated my life to helping others with software, and to do this effectively, I will have to overcome my obstacle.
