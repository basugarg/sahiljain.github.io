WHAT IS THE MOVEMENT? 

The movement is grounded in a Servant Leadership philosophy that encourages leaders to lead by serving others. We believe if we can get people thinking, “What Can I Do For You?”, relationships, workplaces, and society is transformed for the better. When people start thinking about what they can do for others, they start to discover firstly what it is they do or what it is they are passionate about. Following that passion, their individual happiness increases and their relationships are strengthened. And consequently, new ideas in the form of innovation and social impacts are brought to the world!

HOW IT STARTED? 

What started as an innocent Facebook status on the couch with 3 friends, turned into something much larger when it went viral in as little as 2 weeks. At the end of the first day of the initial status asking friends, “WhatCanIDoForYou? #imserious”, the page had reached 5,000 people. By the second week that number had grown to over 50,000, and today the movement has managed to reach well beyond 100,000 people in 15 countries through social media! ( Btw, it only started the second week of the new year)

WHAT WE DO

We run individualized and unique Social Media initiatives, Events, and Seminars in Workplaces/Schools

EVENT @ UNIVERSITY OF WATERLOO

On Thursday March 6th, 2014, the JDI team ran a campaign at the University of Waterloo, where students Tweeted us requests of things we could do for them. Our team of  25+ volunteers located them around campus and did it for them.

Read our feature on the Uwaterloo Life Blog: https://uwaterloo.ca/student-success/blog/post/making-change-one-favour-time

Outcomes of the Event:

  • Interacted with 300+ people
  • Gave out more than 250 chocolates/cookies
  • Distributed 100 Litres of Hot Chocolate
  • Caught the eyes of Humber College and UWestern journalists
  • Approached to start an ambassador program so an event like this happens every semester!
