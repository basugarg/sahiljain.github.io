New Series: #7Days

March 14, 2014

I’m SUPER EXCITED to be shooting a new video series called #7DAYS! The series will highlight how it feels to be in another person’s shoes for 7 days. Sometimes we aren’t able to empathize with people due to social stigmas, authority-relationships, and/or stereotypes, and I hope this series will shatter those barriers.
